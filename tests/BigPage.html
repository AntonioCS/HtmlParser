One Punch Man

Author(s): MURATA Yuusuke, ONE
Released: 2012
Genre(s): Action, Comedy, Drama, Sci-fi, Seinen, Slice of Life, Supernatural
Status: Ongoing (Scan), Ongoing (Publish)
Description: Follows the life of an average hero who manages to win all of his battles with only one punch! This ends up being the cause of a lot of frustration as he no longer feels the thrill and adrenaline of fighting a tough battle. Maybe all of the hard training to become strong wasn't worth it. After all what's so good about having an overwhelming power?
